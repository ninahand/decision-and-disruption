DECISION + DISRUPTION:

A journey to the tate mo dern

A focus on "decision" and "disruption" came out of a general observation of "textures," "bad signs," "frequencies," "detours," and "orientation".

"Decision" we categorized as people orienting themselves on the street, more often than not, right as they stepped out of the tube. "Disruption" we understood as signage + barriers that interrupted our journey, moving us elsewhere.
